Hello!

My name is Adrian. I am a videographer and creative professional hailing from the Garden State. I specialize in promotional and interview video productions and am currently building a web design portfolio (I designed this website!). My journey into video first began in 2007 as a side passion project while studying music at the University of the Arts. I still play a mean trumpet in my spare time and teach children's piano!

Video is essential to modern marketing practices. This makes understanding how to properly implement video assets a high priority. With each project, my objective is to produce quality content that brings value to my clients, handling all phases of production and post-production. Let me help you bring your vision to life!
